Meet Amy

A Professional Plumber with 25 Years of Experience

Amy started her plumbing journey after completing her vocational training in 1998. With a passion for solving complex plumbing issues, she quickly gained recognition for her attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

As the owner of Amy's Plumbing, she personally oversees every project, ensuring that customers receive the highest quality service. Amy is licensed and insured, and brings her extensive knowledge to every job, from simple repairs to complete plumbing renovations.

Over the last 25 years, Amy has built a team of skilled professionals who share her dedication to excellence. Together, they've helped thousands of homeowners and businesses across Broward County with their plumbing needs.
